# Pulsar upload and recording notice

Version 1.0. Canonical policy links:
https://barycenter.live/legal/terms and
https://barycenter.live/legal/content-guidelines.

## U-01. Rights confirmation

By recording, uploading or sending, you confirm that you created this content
or have the rights, permissions and recording consents needed to process it and
share it with every selected recipient. Do not send illegal, harmful,
privacy-invasive, infringing or commercial-music rebroadcast content.

## U-02. Processing and recipients

Pulsar sends only to the recipients shown before submission. The coordinator
can read, validate and normalize the audio: Phase 1 is not end-to-end
encrypted. A recipient may still hear, record or copy what they receive.

## U-03. Retention and controls

Ready audio normally expires within seven days. You can delete your clip;
Pulsar revokes new access immediately and cleans bytes asynchronously, subject
to a reported-evidence hold, legal requirements and database recovery metadata
retained for up to seven days. Recipients can report the clip and block its
sender.

## U-04. Consent action

Use **Record**, **Upload** or **Send** only if you agree to this notice, the
Pulsar Terms, Content Guidelines and Privacy Policy. Cancel to keep the content
local.
