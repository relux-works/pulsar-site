# Pulsar Support and Safety Contacts

Version 1.0. Effective and last updated: 14 July 2026.
Canonical URL: https://barycenter.live/legal/support

English is the controlling version. A Russian translation is available from
the language switch on the public page.

## S-01. Product support

For installation, pairing, playback, delivery, deletion or accountless
credential problems, email support@barycenter.live. Include the Pulsar version,
operating system, approximate time and a short description of what happened.
Do not send passwords, pairing codes, recovery credentials or unnecessary
audio. We may ask for a sanitized diagnostic identifier when it is needed to
find the relevant service event.

## S-02. Reports and moderation

For an ordinary content, conduct, copyright, privacy or safety report, use the
in-product Report action when it is available or email
moderator@barycenter.live. Identify the affected delivery as precisely as you
can and explain the requested action. The normal response target is two
business days during Monday-Friday 10:00-19:00 GMT+4 coverage. This is a target,
not a guarantee that every investigation or remedy will finish in that time.

## S-03. Urgent removal and immediate danger

For apparent child exploitation, a credible imminent threat, non-consensual
intimate material or another urgent removal request, email
moderation-urgent@barycenter.live. The urgent response target is 24 hours. If
someone is in immediate danger, contact the competent local emergency service;
Pulsar is not an emergency service and the mailbox is not continuously
monitored.

## S-04. Privacy and legal requests

Send a privacy rights or data-handling request to privacy@relux.works. Send a
formal legal notice to legal@relux.works. Describe the installation,
Barycenter, transmission or report that lets us locate the relevant record,
but do not disclose another person's credentials. We may need to verify your
authority before disclosing, correcting or deleting data.
